Aradhya Aanjna, born on 16/05/2016, is a 9-year-old Gurgaon based actor. She has been acting since her 2018 debut in the short film ‘Child Alone’ by Aaj Tak group. A sought-after talent in television commercials, she has worked with major brands like P&G, Cadbury, Whirlpool, Tanishq, Realme, Pears, Horlicks, L&T, Asian Paints, Byjus, Mother Dairy, Colgate, and Oriflame. In recent news, she is part of the casts for the Sony Liv web series ‘Potluck’ and ‘The Good Bad Girl’, as well as the Hotstar series ‘Guilty Minds’. Aradhya is featured in the Netflix project ‘Kaala Paani,’ which was released on 18th November, 2023 and she recently completed shooting for the Malayalam movie “Nadikar.” Notably, she starred in the high-profile Jio Home campaign (2025), working alongside cricket players from Mumbai Indians, Punjab Kings, and RCB.
